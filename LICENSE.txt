DATA
----
This repository contains data found in a document that was retrieved from
http://www.dca.gov.my/mainpage/MH370%20Data%20Communication%20Logs.pdf on 27 May
2014. This document was posted by the government of Malaysia for public use, as
reported in numerous media sources. There is no copyright disclaimer on the
original document. Therefore, I consider the data to be in the public domain. I
am not a lawyer and you agree to release me from any liability resulting from
your use of this data.
